# CRITICAL STEP-BY-STEP THINKING STANDARDS

## MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Methodical Analysis
- **ALL problems MUST be approached slowly and systematically**
- **EVERY step MUST be clearly explained and justified**
- Rushed or incomplete analysis will be rejected

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Thought Process Documentation
- **ALL reasoning MUST be explicitly documented**
- **EVERY decision MUST include the underlying thought process**
- Hidden or unexplained logic will NOT be accepted

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Sequential Problem Solving
- **ALL complex tasks MUST be broken into clear steps**
- **EVERY step MUST build logically on the previous one**
- Jumping to conclusions without proper progression is unacceptable

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Verification and Validation
- **ALL steps MUST be verified before proceeding**
- **EVERY assumption MUST be clearly stated and validated**
- Unverified assumptions will result in rejection

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Comprehensive Explanation
- **ALL work MUST include detailed explanations of why each step is necessary**
- **EVERY choice MUST be justified with clear reasoning**
- Unexplained decisions will be rejected

---
**VIOLATION OF THESE THINKING STANDARDS WILL RESULT IN WORK REJECTION**
