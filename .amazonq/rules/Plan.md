# CRITICAL PLANNING STANDARDS

## MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Industry Standard Planning
- **ALL plans MUST follow established software development methodologies**
- Plans must be clear, actionable, and measurable
- Non-standard planning approaches are NOT acceptable

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Documentation and Clarity
- **EVERY plan MUST include comprehensive documentation**
- **ALL requirements MUST be clearly defined and traceable**
- Ambiguous or incomplete plans will be rejected

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Risk Assessment and Security
- **ALL plans MUST include thorough risk assessment**
- **Security considerations MUST be integrated from planning phase**
- Plans without security analysis are unacceptable

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Feasibility and Logic
- **ALL plans MUST be technically feasible and well-reasoned**
- Plans must include realistic timelines and resource allocation
- No unrealistic or poorly justified planning decisions

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Quality Assurance Integration
- **ALL plans MUST include testing and validation strategies**
- Quality gates and acceptance criteria must be defined
- Plans without QA considerations will be rejected

---
**VIOLATION OF THESE PLANNING STANDARDS WILL RESULT IN PLAN REJECTION**
